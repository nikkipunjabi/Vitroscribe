Vitroscribe

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 14, 2026

Introduction

Vitroscribe ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. Vitroscribe is a privacy-first macOS application designed to transcribe meetings entirely on your local device. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our application and connect to third-party services like Google Calendar.

1. Data Processing (Privacy-First Architecture)

Critical Privacy Note: Vitroscribe is designed so that your sensitive data never leaves your computer.

  • Audio & Transcripts: All audio recording and speech-to-text transcription are performed locally on your Mac using Apple's SFSpeechRecognizer. No audio or transcript data is ever uploaded to our servers or any third-party cloud.
  • Local Storage: Your transcripts and meeting history are stored in a local SQLite database on your device's hard drive.

2. Google API Data Usage

When you connect your Google Calendar, Vitroscribe requests read-only access to your calendar events.

  • How we use this data: We fetch upcoming events to identify meeting links (Google Meet, Zoom, Teams) and display them in your dashboard. This allows the app to provide the "Join & Capture" HUD.
  • Storage: We do not store your Google Calendar data on any external servers. This data is only stored locally on your device to facilitate app features.
  • Data Sharing: We do not sell, trade, or share your Google user data with any third parties.

Google Limited Use Disclosure: Vitroscribe's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Service User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

3. Permissions

Vitroscribe requires the following permissions to function:

  • Microphone: To capture meeting audio for local transcription.
  • Speech Recognition: To convert audio to text on-device.
  • Calendar: To sync your schedule (via Google/Microsoft OAuth).
  • Automation/Accessibility: To detect meeting windows in your browser.

4. Third-Party Services

Vitroscribe uses Google and Microsoft OAuth for authentication. These services may collect information as governed by their respective privacy policies.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
