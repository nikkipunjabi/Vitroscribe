Workflow Redefined

Everything you need to capture knowledge without lifting a finger.

100% On-Device Privacy

No audio or text ever leaves your Mac. Vitroscribe uses WhisperKit with Apple Core ML for multilingual, offline transcription — no internet required.

Smart Calendar Sync

Connect Google Calendar and Microsoft Outlook to automatically fetch meeting links. Join and start recording with a single click from the HUD or menu bar.

Autonomous Detection

Automatically spots when you join Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, Slack, or Webex — and starts recording without any manual action.

Lives in Your Menu Bar

Runs quietly in the background as a native menu bar app. Start or stop recording, see your next meeting, and open the full app — all from the top of your screen.

Meeting Reminders & HUD

Floating join prompts appear before each meeting with a one-click "Join & Capture" action. Multiple concurrent meeting reminders are stacked and shown simultaneously.

Global Search & History

Instantly find any transcript by searching titles or content. Your entire meeting archive lives locally in a SQLite database on your device.
